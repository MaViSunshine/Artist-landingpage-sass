About

Started creating art pieces from a young age and made my first art exibit at 16. It was then when I sold my first painting and decided to study art.

Graduated from Art School in 2003 and started teaching in 2005 in academic settings. In 2009 started teaching private lessons and workshop to students of all ages.

My artistic style

Most of my pieces are colorful abstract paintings with lots of texture (but I also make pieces in other styles upon request).

I enjoy the results of that style, as due to shadows and pareidolia, it looks different acording to placing and the interpretation of the beholder.
